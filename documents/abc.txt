Company Name: TechCorp India

Leave Policy:
- Every employee gets 20 paid leaves per year.
- Sick leaves are 10 days per year.
- Casual leaves are 5 days per year.
- Leaves can be carried forward to next year up to 10 days.

Work From Home Policy:
- Employees can work from home on Fridays.
- Work from home requires manager approval.
- Maximum 2 days work from home per week.

Office Timings:
- Office hours are 9 AM to 6 PM.
- Flexible timing allowed between 8 AM to 10 AM login.
- Lunch break is 1 PM to 2 PM.

Salary Policy:
- Salaries are credited on 1st of every month.
- Bonus is given twice a year in June and December.
- Increment cycle is every April.

Probation Policy:
- New employees have 3 months probation period.
- No leaves allowed during probation.
- Performance review at end of probation.
